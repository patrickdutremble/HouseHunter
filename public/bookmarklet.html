How to install

  1. Make sure the bookmarks bar is visible (Chrome/Edge: Ctrl+Shift+B).
  2. Click and hold one of the blue buttons above, then drag it up to the bookmarks bar and release.
  3. You should see a new bookmark named "Add to HouseHunter".
  4. Visit any Centris listing page and click the bookmark. A new tab will open confirming the listing was added.

What it captures

Centris link, location (city), property type (Condo/House/...), asking price, taxes, fees, bedrooms, area, parking, year built, coordinates (for commute calc), and listing photo. Everything else is filled in later.
